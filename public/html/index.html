Bitponics is an open source networked home hydroponics monitoring system. It tracks light, humidity, temperature, pH, and PPM and transmits these readings via WIFI to a web database. Once online, you are able to analyze and visualize this data from a web dashboard. The interface also controls the intervals of pumps and lighting in your system. It is geared to educate and give a better understanding of how plants grow and function. The system is currently in development, so please contact or follow us on twitter to be notified of updates.
